NMG-VRG Launches New Research Projects

Published on 23rd March 2026

NMG-VRG is pleased to announce the launch of new research projects on 23 March 2026 as part of its commitment to advancing microbial genomics, computational biology, and global health research.

These projects provide opportunities for students, early-career researchers, and young professionals to participate in collaborative, project-based research across key thematic areas including antimicrobial resistance genomics, pathogen surveillance, comparative genomics, metagenomics, artificial intelligence applications, and One Health.

Through these newly launched projects, members will engage in scientific discussions, data-driven investigations, skill development, and collaborative knowledge generation within an international virtual research environment.
