Authorship & Data Policy

Authorship within NMG-VRG is based on meaningful intellectual contribution, active participation, and agreed project responsibilities. Contributors are appropriately acknowledged in all outputs.

Data generated within the network is managed responsibly and shared internally or externally only with the necessary approval and governance processes. Members are expected to uphold confidentiality, integrity, and responsible research conduct.
